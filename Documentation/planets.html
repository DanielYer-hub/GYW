CAMPAIGN PLANETS

Forge World

Start Battle with 2 CP.

Temple World

Once Per Battle. At Your Command Phase until Your next Command Phase. Choose three units in your army. This Units receive +1 to their Save Characteristic.

Fortress World

Once Per Battle. At Your Command Phase until Your next Command Phase. Choose three units in your army. This Units receive +1 to their Toughness Characteristic.

Colonial World

The player starts the Battle with 2 Victory Points.

Civilized World

Allows you to replace or select another Detachment.

World of Pleasures

Once per Battle. At the start of Your Command Phase until Your next Command Phase, give 5 FNP for two Units (excluding Monster, Vechicle ).

Incursion.
Strike Force.

The World in the Eye of Horror

In any round, a player can remove 1 Objective Marker and return it in the next round (Once per Battle).

Incursion.
Strike Force.

Craft World

Allows you to change the terrain layout at Your Deployment Zone or in No Man's Land (Up to 2 parts of terrain exluding: Ruine, Fortification).

Incursion.
Strike Force.

Hive World

Allows you to replace 1 unit in the roster.

Incursion.
Strike Force.

Spaceport

If you reach 1500 pts then you get one Spaceport. After that, when you reach 2000 pts, you have the option to get another Spaceport. The Spaceport gives you the opportunity to get one Epic Hero and thus replace any Unit except Warlord in your army that does not exceed the number of your pts.
