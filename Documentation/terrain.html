Campaign Terrain

All the rules of the terrain, its use, and the visibility of units remain the same as prescribed in the core book (with the exception of some narrative refinements).
For Terrain set up, you can use different sources or do it however you want.

Fortification

All Infantry Units get Benefit of Cover and all Shooting Attacks against this Unit subtract -1 to Hit-roll and Wound-roll. (Important: you can't perferm a charge instead this terrain. You can't finish a full Move instead this terrain, if you start the Move and part of your move was instead this terrain you imidiatly must finish your Move on this part of terrain: excluding for Fly).

Ruine

Gives your Unit Benefit of Cover. (Important: if you play with a Ruine which has a windows, and models stay inside, they are visible for Shooting Actions).

Trash

All Movements through this terrain subtract -2 from the Move Characteristic.

Forest

Any unit shooting through the forest from any parts of battlefield including terrain subtracts -1 to Hit-roll (Infinite Height).

Barricades

If your unit is within 1" of the Barricade, all Shooting Attacks against it subtract -1 to Wound-roll. (Does not apply if the barricade is not between attacker and defender).

Craters

Gives your Unit Benefit of Cover.

Decorations

Can serve as a continuation of terrain if placed closely and gives the same effect. You cannot finish a Move on it.

Heavy Terrain

The terrain designated as "Heavy Terrain" at the start of the game will not have the "Infinite Height" property. This means that this terrain completely defines its size and width, providing a blocking effect. In other words, if an opponent is on a different height or on the opposite side of the terrain and can see your models, they will be considered eligible for shooting. However, you cannot perform a charge or move through this terrain . Therefore, it can only be bypassed or fly over.

Statues

During your Shooting Phase, choose a unit or a model and make attacks at the statue. If you get two 6+ hits, lay the statue down in any direction and use it as a barricade.
