Campaign Story

A universe where there is only war creates hundreds of reasons for it to begin. The Lacrius System became another picture of disaster. Once rich in minerals, it existed untouched for long millennia - until the Imperium's war machine came. Worlds began to empty, resources depleted.But the Imperium was not the only one who cast their eyes on the paradise worlds. Ever seeking the glory of their Gods, the armies of Chaos invaded Lacrius to perform dark rituals in the name of their masters. The Xenos armies followed. Each of the aliens sought their own gain: some were eager for battle, while others wanted to recapture virgin worlds to settle them.At present, the fate of the Lacrius system is not predetermined, since neither side is going to give up its rights.
