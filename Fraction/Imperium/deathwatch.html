DEATHWATCH


Faction Mission:

We will stop the enemy before he shows himself.
At the End of the Battle destroy more opponent Units then your Units.

Conditions:

* At the end Battle:
* If Your PTS on table more than Opponent.
* Success - 1 point of Duty.
* If at the end of the Battle you controll your Objective at your Deployment Zone and Objective at Opponent Deployment Zone.
* Success - 2 points of Duty.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 1 point of Duty: you can replace until two Units at your army.
* 3 points of Duty: you can replace all Units at your army.
