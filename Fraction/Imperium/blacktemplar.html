BLACK TEMPLARS


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual in the name of the Emperor.
Destroy enemy Character.

Conditions:

*For each enemy Character - 1 point of oath.
*For each Warlord - 2 points of oath.

Restriction:

*If opponent has only one Character at the army - 3 points of oath.

Rewards:

If you earned 3 points of oath. - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received by the warlord for the next Battle:
*1-2-3: Get on the unit that the hero leads including the hero all Melee Weapons get Precision.
*4-5-6: Once per round, you can use a Stratagems that has already been used twice.
