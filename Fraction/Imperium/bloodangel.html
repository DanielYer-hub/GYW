BLOOD ANGELS


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual of Blood.
Destroy enemy Units at Engagement Range.

Conditions:

*For each enemy unit destroyed - 1 point of rage.
*If an enemy unit was destroyed by a Death Company - 2 points of rage.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned 5 points of rage - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received by the warlord for the next Battle:
*1-2-3: Once per Battle, one Jump Pack Unit in Reserves can be Set-Up at '3' from the enemy Units.
*4-5-6: All Units which have FNP increase this stat. +1.
