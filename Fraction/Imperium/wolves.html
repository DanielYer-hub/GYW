Space Wolves


Faction Mission:

For the All-Father and Fenris!
Character or Character with Unit which he lead - destroy opponent Units on Objective or opponent Deployment Zone.

Conditions:

* At the end Battle Round:
* If Character or Character with Unit which he lead - destroyed more opponent Units then opponent destroyed your Units on Objective.
* Success - 1 point of Russ.
* If Character or Character with Unit which he lead - destroyed more opponent Units then opponent destroyed your Units in Deployment Zone.
* Success - 2 points of Russ.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points of Russ: all core Stratagems for opponent army which stay at '6' from Space Wolves Character increas by 1CP.
* 4 points of Russ: all Faction Stratagems for opponent army which stay at '6' from Space Wolves Character increas by 1CP.
