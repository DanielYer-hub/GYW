Faction Mission:

This world belongs to the emperor.
Third Round - all Units (excluding Vehicle) perferm Action at Shooting Phase on Objective.

Conditions:

* 3-rd Round: if you controll 1 Objective receive 1 point.
* 4-th Round: if you controll 2 Objective receive 2 points.
* 5-th Round: if you controll more Objective then your opponent receive 3 points.

Restriction:

* Not in Your Deployment Zone.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 3 points : 1 Unit (excluding Vehicle) get Deep Strike ability.
* 6 points : Before Battle you can remove more 100 pts to Strategik Reservers.
