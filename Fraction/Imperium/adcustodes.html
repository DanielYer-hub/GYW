ADEPTA CUSTODES


Faction Mission:

We do not have the right to let him down again!
Second Round (Command Phase) - Choose one Unit from Your army.
He becomes the Keeper of Shadows.

Conditions:

* At the end Battle Round:
* If this unit was not Destroy.
* Success - 1 point "His Glance".
* At the end of the Battle if you controll Objective Marker at Opponent Deployment Zone.
* Success - 2 points "His Glance".

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned 4 points "His Glance" - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received for the next Battle:
* 1-2-3: At the end of Deployment Step, you can choose two units from opponent army and move them on '6' in any direction in their Deployment Zone.
* 4-5-6: Once per Round you can use 1 Stratagem from another Detachment.
