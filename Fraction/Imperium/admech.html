ADEPTA MECHANICUS


Faction Mission:

In search of the ancient STC (Standard Template Construct).
Any Unit - from the Second Round should controll Objective at No Man's Land.

Conditions:

* At the end of Battle Round if you stay controll Objective roll 1D6:
* Success - 1 point of STC.

Restriction:

With each round, if you control the same Objective, improve your roll by 1.
* 2-nd Round 1-st time: 5+.
* 3-rd Round 2-nd time: 4+.
* 4-th Round 3-rd time: 3+.
* 5-th Round 4-th time: 2+.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points - you can set up one additional Unit to Strategik Reservers ignoring number of PTS on the army format (not in Engagement Range).
* 3 points - for one Unit receive 4+ invulnerable save.
* 4 points - one Unit get '4' for Range Attack .
