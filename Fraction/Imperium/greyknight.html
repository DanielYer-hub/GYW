GREY KNIGHTS


Faction Mission:

We are the Emperor Shield.
Second Round - Warlord or Warlord Unit destroy opponent Unit.

Conditions:

* If you destroyed opponent Unit:
* Roll your leadership Test.
* Success - 1 point of Titan.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 3 points of Titan: At the start of the Battle you can move one Objective at No Man's Land on '3' closer to Your Deployment.
* 5 points of Titan: Once per Battle you can choose one Objective at No Man's Land and blocked it, until the end of the Round this Objective does't bring VP.
