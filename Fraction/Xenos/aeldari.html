AELDARI


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual on Objectives.
Find and return the stones of the soul.

Conditions:

*Action - at your Shooting Phase, Infantry Unit which stand on Objective Marker throw 1D6.(Not Controll)
*At Your Deployment On 4+ - the action is successful, receive 1 soul stone.
*At No Man's Land On 3+ - the action is successful, receive 1 soul stones.
*At Enemy Deployment On 2+ - the action is successful, receive 1 soul stones.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned 3 soul stones - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received by one Unit for the next Battle:
*1-2-3: +1 Attack for all Weapon Characteristic.
*4-5-6: 4+ Invulnerable Save.
