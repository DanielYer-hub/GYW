AELDARI


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual on Objectives at No Man's Land or Opponent Drop Zone.
Find and return the stones of the soul.

Conditions:

Action - at your Shooting Phase, Psyker Unit which stand on Objective Marker throw 1D6.
On 4+ - the action is successful, receive 1 soul stone.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned 3 soul stones - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received by the warlord for the next Battle:
1-2-3: +1 Attack for all Weapon Characteristic.
4-5-6: Once per Battle, the Warlord can ignore any damage from 1 attack.
