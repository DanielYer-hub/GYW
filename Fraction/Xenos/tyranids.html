TYRANIDS


Faction Mission:

Absorb all biomass.
Monster Unit - stand at '9' from the edge in quarters.

Conditions:

* At the end Movement Phase roll Leadership Test for this Unit.
* Success - receive 1 point of biomass.
* The success quarters is becoming uninhabitable - all units in it do not receive Cover at any conditions.

Restriction:

* This Unit must not be Battle Shock.
* Not in your Deployment Zone

Rewards:

If you earned 3 points of biomass:
* For the next battle you can add 50 PTS.
(Important: if you don't receive 3 points of biomass, You must exclude these 50 PTS).
