TAU EMPIRE


Faction Mission:

Superiority at a distance.
Destroy opponent Units at Shooting Phase.

Conditions:

* If you destroy one opponent Units by range Weapon:
* Recieve 1 points.
* If opponent lose more Units then you at this round:
* Recieve 1 point.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 5 points - Experimental Weaponry - choose one squad. Improve one characteristic of one ranged weapon for that squad by 1 in this battle. The profile gains the keyword 'Hazardous'
* 7 points - Pre-Production Trials - choose one ranged weapon profile. Improve one characteristic of that profile by 1 for all squads using it in this battle. The profile gains the keyword 'Hazardous'.
* Profile Weapon: (AP, Damage, Range, Attacks, BS)
