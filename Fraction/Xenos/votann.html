LEAGUES OF VOTANN


Faction Mission:

The power of ancestors is in our depths.
At Command Phase - Choose one Objective at No Man's Land and one Objective at your Deployment Zone.

Conditions:

*If you controll both Objective until end of your turn.
*Success - 1 point of memory.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points of memory: 1 Infantry Unit receive Assult for Range Weapon.
* 3 points of memory: 2 Infantry Units receive Assult for Range Weapon.
* 4 points of memory: 3 Infantry Units receive Assult for Range Weapon.
