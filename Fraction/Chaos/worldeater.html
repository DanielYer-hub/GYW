WORLD EATERS


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual for the Throne of Skulls.
Khorne Berzerks - destroy Infantry Units.

Conditions:

* For each destroyed Unit in close combat, roll a number of D6 equal to the number of models in the destroyed unit.
* On a 4+, collect their skulls.

Restriction:

None.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 10 skulls - add. '2' to Move Characteristic of Khorne Berzerks.
* 20 skulls - add. +1 Damage to Khorne Berzerks and Character all Weapons.
