DEATH GUARD


Faction Mission:

Perform a ritual of 7 diseases at the name of Grandfather.
Enemy unit placed at the range Death Guard aura by Terminators or Plague Marines.

Conditions:

* At the end of Shooting Phase.
* A Death Guard player, must pass the opponent's leadership test.
* Success - 1 point of the disease.

Restriction:

* Just 1 enemy Unit at the aura range per Terminator or Plague Marines.

Rewards:

If you earned 7 points of the disease - roll 1D6, the fallen bonus will be received for the next Battle:
* 1-2-3 - receive speed of Spawn for Plague Marines and Terminator Units, add '1' to Move Characteristic.
* 4-5-6 - Plague Marines and Terminators could re-roll Wound-roll for all Bolter Weapon.
