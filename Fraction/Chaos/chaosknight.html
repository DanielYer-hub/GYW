CHAOS KNIGHT


Faction Mission:

To ruin this world and betray it to fire.
Shooting Phase Action: 1 Objective Per turn at No Man's Land.

Conditions:

* If you perferm Action, roll 1D6 on 4+ remove this Objective untill end of the Battle.
* Success - 1 point of Ruine.
* If you perfem Action at opponent Deployment Zone:
* Success - 2 points of Ruine.

Restriction:

* If you have a Primary with a same conditions, combine it.
* You perferm Action for Primary and roll for your Faction Mission.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points of Ruine: 1 Wardog model receive +1 for OC.
* 4 points of Ruine: 1 Wardog model receive +2 for OC.
