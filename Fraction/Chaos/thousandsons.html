THOUSAND SONS


Faction Mission:

See us and grant us changes.
Psyker Unit - must Perfem a Ritual at the centre of Battlefield .

Conditions:

* At the End of Command phase roll 1D6 on 4+:
* Success - 1 point of Changes.

Restriction:

* Just one Psyker at one Turn.
* If you want repeit the Ritual, you must use another Psyker.
* Must have one Battle round between rituals per Psyker

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points of Changes: You choose who will Attacker or Defender.
* 3 points of Changes: choose one of this option.
(1) Roll 1D6 on 3+: opponenet can't Conquer your Planet.
(2) Roll 1D6 on 5+: you transfer to the opponent's planet at their choice and you are already fighting for the opponent's Planet.
