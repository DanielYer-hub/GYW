THOUSAND SONS


Faction Mission:

See us and grant us changes.
Psyker Unit - must Perfem a Ritual at the centre of Battlefield .

Conditions:

* At the End of Command phase roll 1D6 on 4+:
* Success - 1 point of Changes.

Restriction:

* Just one Psyker at one Turn.
* If you want repeit the Ritual, you must use another Psyker.

Rewards:

If you earned:
* 2 points of Changes: You choose who will go First without roll.
* 3 points of Changes: add +1 to roll who will Attacker or Defender (if you won and you Defend your World at the end of the Battle, opponent can't conquer your World).
